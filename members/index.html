Code Island Leadership

Ryan Kelly, Co-captain—Ryan is the Urban Information Specialist at the Providence Plan, transforming a wide range of public and private data sets into usable information displays that help residents and outsiders understand that complexity that is Providence, RI

David Johnson, Co-captain—David is a “white hat” consultant in the critical but secretive world of information security, working with governments and corporations to keep the forces of evil from exploiting the openness that defines a civil society

Shawn Selleck, Community Organizer—Shawn is an civic innovation consultant to the City of Providence, directly working to make public data accessible to the public

John Speck, Storyteller—John is a communications consultant and civic activist, who blogs under the pseudonym Frymaster

Thom Guertin, Government Liaison—Thom is Rhode Island’s Chief Digital Officer, leading RI’s Office of Digital Excellence; he helps Code Island gain access to data from state-level agencies
