Code Island is the official Code for America brigade in Rhode Island. We are an all-volunteer organization of people looking to improve our community through technology.

As a general rule, we hold a meetup the first and third Tuesday of every month at 6pm at Brown University’s TRI Lab at 10 Davol Square. Our meetups are free and open to anyone who wants to contribute.
